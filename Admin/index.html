Hi, I’m [Your Name]

I’m 38. When I was 14, I wrote a top 100 song list in a spiral notebook. I’ve updated it every year since — cutting songs that no longer hit, adding new tracks that unlock forgotten memories.

This podcast is not about music criticism. It’s about the stories we attach to songs. The 7th grade dance, the cross-country road trip, the hard breakup — the moments that would fade if not for a 3-minute track.

Every week, I chat with guests who have made their own top 100 lists. We debate rankings, share the memories behind their #1 song, and talk about why maintaining a list is the most rewarding tedious project you will ever commit to.
